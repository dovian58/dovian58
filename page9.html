about us

Welcome to the world of DOVIAN 58, where business meets innovation! We are your trusted experts for the design, sale and rental of equipment and machinery. We don't just sell, we make your life easier and more fun!

With an overwhelming passion for machines and a keen eye for our customers' needs, we are committed to providing only the best. Our headquarters are located at Via Primo Maggio, 63 in Rovato (BS), where our team is always ready to answer any questions you may have. Do not hesitate to contact us!
