Do you need reliable and powerful excavators for your next project? Dovian58 offers an excavator rental service that guarantees high-quality machines ready to meet all your needs. 20m aerial platform

The 20m aerial platform is an essential piece of equipment for working at height safely and efficiently. Equipped with a boom that can be extended up to 20 metres, this platform is ideal for maintenance, construction and cleaning work. Easy to manoeuvre and with state-of-the-art safety systems, it guarantees optimal stability even at great heights.

cte traces model 135 elevating platform

The cte traces model 135 lifting platform, year 2018, with only 1135 hours of use, is a versatile and reliable solution for lifting needs in construction sites and work at height. Equipped with advanced features and built with high quality materials, this machine ensures safety and high performance for the operator.

crane

The used crane truck is an excellent solution for those looking for a low-cost alternative for heavy lifting and handling operations. Perfect for construction sites, warehouses and other industrial applications, it combines efficiency and reliability.
